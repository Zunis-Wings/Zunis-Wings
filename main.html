Our Story

Zuni's is a small family-owned restaurant that offers many dishes to the Midlands area of South Carolina. Of our offerings, our speciality is our Korean dishes and crispy, Chinese-style wings as we make them using our own special recipes. All dishes are cooked to order using the freshest of ingredients. Come in and try us today!
