Our Story

Welcome to Zuni's, a charming family-owned eatery nestled in the heart of the Midlands, South Carolina. We take pride in offering a diverse array of mouthwatering dishes to tantalize your taste buds.

Our culinary passion shines through in our signature Korean delicacies and our delectably crispy Chinese-style wings, each crafted with care using our time-honored secret recipes. Dedicated to freshness and flavor, we prepare each dish to order, ensuring that every bite bursts with the vibrant taste of premium ingredients.

Experience the warmth of our hospitality, the richness of our flavors, and the satisfaction of a truly unforgettable dining experience at Zuni's!
